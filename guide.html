🟢

You're a slime! You spawn in the village center. Click trees, rocks, or fishing spots to start gathering resources. Sell items at the General Store for coins. Level up skills, craft equipment, and take on bosses.

First goal: buy Stone Tools at the Blacksmith for 50 coins — 25% faster gathering speed!

Star Enhancement

Right-click worn gear → Enhance

Items go up to 10 stars, boosting ATK and DEF. A timing minigame controls bonus success chance — stop the slider in the green zone.

Stars 7+ can destroy your item on failure! If destroyed, you get a broken version of the item back (same tier, no stars). Enhance carefully.

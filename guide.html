SlimeVilleWiki

Play

You're a slime! You spawn in the village center. Click trees, rocks, or fishing spots to start gathering resources. Sell items at the General Store for coins. Level up skills, craft equipment, and take on bosses.

First goal: buy Stone Tools at the Blacksmith for 100 coins!

Level Progression

How leveling improves your character

Hover over the graph to see values
🌲⛏️
🐟

Click a resource node to gather. Each success gives XP and an item. Your bag holds 32 slots.

Gathering has a fail chance that shrinks as you level — 50% at level 1, nearly 0% at high levels. Foraging and Flax never fail.

All Skills

10 trainable skills • No level cap (slows after 200)

XP Formula: Level L requires 34 × (L−1)² total XP. After level 200, progression slows to 10×.

Fail Chance: Gathering starts at 50% fail rate at level 1, drops steadily through mid-levels (~19% at 100) and tapers to 2% at level 200.
